Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 4th March 2025

Miss Lauren Riley Ltd. ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data when you visit our website or interact with our services.

1. Data We Collect

We may collect the following personal data:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, business details (if provided).
  • Payment Details: For processing purchases (via secure third-party payment providers).
  • Usage Data: Information about your use of our website (e.g., IP address, browser type, cookies).
  • Communication Data: Information from emails, messages, or forms you complete.

2. How We Collect Data

  • Directly from you when you fill out forms, book a discovery call, or subscribe to our services.
  • Automatically via cookies and analytics tools when you visit our website.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and use your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide coaching services and manage your bookings.
  • To communicate with you regarding inquiries or services.
  • To process payments securely.
  • To improve our website and marketing efforts.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing Your Data

We will never sell your data. However, we may share your data with trusted third parties:

  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) to handle transactions.
  • Email marketing services (e.g., Mailchimp) for newsletters or updates.
  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics) to improve website performance.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined above or as required by law.

6. Your Rights

Under the UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw your consent to data processing at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you believe your data rights are violated.

7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. For more details, please see our Cookies Policy.
