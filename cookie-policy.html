Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 4th March 2025

Miss Lauren Riley Ltd. ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website to provide a better user experience, analyze website traffic, and support our marketing efforts. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the website to function properly (e.g., enabling secure login or remembering your privacy preferences).
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website (e.g., which pages are most visited) to help us improve its functionality.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization (e.g., remembering your language preferences).
  • Targeting/Marketing Cookies: These cookies track your browsing habits to deliver relevant advertisements and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions correctly.
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior to improve performance.
  • Personalize your experience by remembering your preferences.
  • Support marketing efforts by delivering tailored content and ads.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party cookies from trusted providers, such as:

  • Google Analytics: To track and analyze website performance and visitor behavior.
  • Social Media Platforms: To enable sharing and interaction with our content (e.g., Instagram, LinkedIn).

These third parties may collect and use data according to their privacy policies.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may impact your experience on our website. For more information on managing cookies, visit www.aboutcookies.org.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or our practices. Please review this page periodically for the latest updates.
